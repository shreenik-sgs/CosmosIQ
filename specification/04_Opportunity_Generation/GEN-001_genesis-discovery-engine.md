# GEN-001 — Genesis Discovery Engine

**Chapter Class:** Opportunity Generation

### Purpose

Genesis discovers opportunities within continuously evolving understanding of reality.

Genesis is the first consumer of EIOS rather than a further chapter of it.

Where the understanding architecture forms scientific and domain understanding, Genesis decides what within that understanding is worth pursuing.

Opportunities are created by changing reality; Genesis discovers them as understanding of that change matures.

The objective is the discovery of opportunity — emerging, evidence-grounded, and continuously revisable — before it becomes obvious.

### Position in the Architecture

The architecture separates Scientific Understanding, Domain Intelligence, and Opportunity Generation into distinct cognitive layers.

Genesis is the layer of Opportunity Generation.

It consumes understanding; it does not form understanding.

Purpose — opportunity, value, and worth — enters the architecture here, and SHALL NEVER flow back down into the layers that form understanding.

Genesis carries its own identifier namespace (GEN) to make this boundary visible: it is the first consumer of the understanding architecture, not an extension of it.

---

### Genesis

Genesis is the opportunity-generation engine.

Genesis SHALL consume the Intelligence Portfolio, the domain Intelligence Assessments, and the Scientific Worldview, and from them discover opportunities.

Genesis SHALL orchestrate across the domain intelligences rather than reproduce any of them.

Genesis SHALL form opportunities; it SHALL NOT form understanding.

---

### The Understanding-to-Opportunity Boundary

Genesis SHALL ground every opportunity in understanding it did not itself form.

Genesis SHALL reference Intelligence Assessments and Intelligence Products by version, and SHALL NEVER recompute, restate, or redefine them.

Where understanding is uncertain, the opportunity grounded upon it SHALL inherit that uncertainty rather than conceal it.

Genesis SHALL NOT reach downward into how understanding is formed.

---

### Opportunity

Opportunity is the canonical object of this layer.

Opportunity is defined canonically in the Knowledge Model (EIOS-002); Genesis owns the semantics and transitions of the Opportunity lifecycle without redefining the object.

An Opportunity represents a possibility worth pursuing, grounded in understanding and carrying explicit confidence, provenance, and uncertainty.

Genesis introduces no new canonical reasoning object; it reasons in Opportunities, grounded in Intelligence Assessments.

---

### Opportunity Hypothesis

An Opportunity Hypothesis is an Opportunity in its candidate state — proposed, not yet adjudicated.

Genesis SHALL generate Opportunity Hypotheses from the Intelligence Portfolio.

Multiple competing Opportunity Hypotheses MAY coexist for the same emerging reality, and SHALL be preserved until evidence supports adjudication.

The Opportunity Hypothesis is how the architecture answers, at last, what is worth pursuing within what it understands.

---

### Opportunity Formation

Genesis SHALL form Opportunity Hypotheses by synthesizing domain Intelligence Assessments across domains.

Opportunity Formation SHALL preserve:

* the grounding Intelligence Assessments and Products
* the participating domains
* the worldview version
* confidence
* uncertainty

Opportunity Formation SHALL remain scientifically explainable.

---

### Cross-Domain Opportunity Synthesis

The strongest opportunities frequently arise where independent domains converge.

Genesis SHALL synthesize opportunities across technology, economic, supply network, capital, and future domain intelligences.

Cross-Domain Opportunity Synthesis SHALL preserve the contribution of each participating domain.

---

### Opportunity Relationships

Opportunities do not exist in isolation; they support, depend upon, compete with, and supersede one another.

Genesis SHALL maintain explicit relationships among Opportunities in an Opportunity Graph.

Nodes represent Opportunities.

Edges represent:

* supports
* depends-on
* competes-with
* supersedes
* complements
* derives-from

The Opportunity Graph SHALL also relate each Opportunity to the Intelligence Assessments that ground it.

The Opportunity Graph SHALL remain continuously evolving and replayable.

---

### Opportunity Timing

Genesis SHALL assess the timing of every opportunity.

Opportunity Timing SHALL ground in the emergence, readiness, and time-to-inflection understanding produced by the underlying layers.

Opportunity Timing SHALL preserve uncertainty, and SHALL express how early an opportunity is recognized relative to broad awareness.

The discovery of opportunity before it becomes obvious is the central purpose of Genesis.

---

### Opportunity Magnitude

Genesis SHALL assess the magnitude of every opportunity.

Magnitude SHALL be grounded in understanding and SHALL always carry explicit uncertainty.

No magnitude SHALL be asserted as certain, and no opportunity SHALL be reduced to a single number.

---

### Opportunity Lifecycle

The lifecycle states of an Opportunity SHALL be owned by Genesis; the Knowledge Model owns the structural lifecycle field, and Genesis owns its values and semantics.

Illustrative lifecycle states include:

* candidate
* substantiated
* active
* weakening
* superseded
* retired

Every state transition SHALL itself become a historical event.

---

### Opportunity Adjudication

Genesis SHALL continuously adjudicate Opportunity Hypotheses as understanding evolves.

An Opportunity MAY be:

* substantiated
* refined
* deferred
* superseded
* rejected

Every adjudication SHALL preserve complete grounding and justification.

---

### Opportunity Evolution

Opportunities SHALL evolve as the understanding beneath them evolves.

When belief revision or validation drift changes the understanding grounding an Opportunity, that Opportunity SHALL be re-evaluated.

An opportunity supported today MAY be overturned tomorrow by stronger understanding; Genesis SHALL preserve the complete evolution rather than discard it.

No Opportunity SHALL silently retain a superseded grounding.

---

### Opportunity Replayability

Every Opportunity SHALL be replayable.

Each Opportunity SHALL bind the versions of the understanding it was formed against, so that its formation can be reconstructed exactly.

Prior versions of an Opportunity SHALL be preserved and SHALL remain replayable.

---

### Opportunity Portfolio

Genesis SHALL maintain Opportunity Portfolios.

An Opportunity Portfolio is a coordinated collection of evolving opportunities under continuous monitoring.

An Opportunity Portfolio is not an investment portfolio; it carries no allocation, no position, and no capital.

Opportunity Portfolios SHALL preserve diversity of opportunity and SHALL remain continuously evolving and replayable.

---

### Opportunity Prioritization

Not every Opportunity carries equal significance.

Genesis SHALL continuously prioritize Opportunities according to their expected significance.

Illustrative prioritization factors include:

* magnitude
* timing
* confidence
* breadth of impact
* strength of grounding
* uncertainty

Prioritization SHALL remain continuously re-evaluable as understanding evolves.

Prioritization SHALL express relative significance, not investment recommendation.

---

### Continuous Opportunity Discovery

Genesis SHALL discover opportunities continuously, not on demand.

As understanding evolves, new opportunities emerge and existing opportunities strengthen, weaken, or dissolve.

Continuous Opportunity Discovery SHALL remain a permanent responsibility of Genesis.

---

### Handoff

Genesis SHALL provide Opportunity Hypotheses and Opportunity Portfolios to downstream investment and decision subsystems.

Genesis SHALL discover what is worth pursuing; it SHALL NOT decide how to act upon it.

Investment, allocation, and position belong to subsystems beyond Genesis.

---

### What Genesis Is Not

Genesis is not an investment engine.

Genesis SHALL NOT allocate capital, select securities, take positions, construct investment portfolios, or execute decisions.

Genesis forms opportunities and hands them onward; acting upon them is the responsibility of consumers beyond this layer.

### Architectural Rules

- **AR-1501** — Genesis SHALL discover opportunities by consuming the understanding produced by the Reality Intelligence and Cognitive Architecture layers; it SHALL form opportunities, never understanding.
- **AR-1502** — Genesis SHALL ground every Opportunity in Intelligence Assessments, Intelligence Products, and the Scientific Worldview, referenced by version, and SHALL NEVER recompute, restate, or redefine them.
- **AR-1503** — Purpose — opportunity, value, and worth — SHALL enter the architecture only at Genesis and SHALL NEVER flow downward into the layers that form understanding.
- **AR-1504** — Genesis SHALL reason in Opportunities, the canonical object defined by the Knowledge Model; it SHALL own the Opportunity lifecycle semantics without redefining the object, and SHALL introduce no new canonical reasoning object.
- **AR-1505** — Genesis SHALL generate Opportunity Hypotheses as candidate Opportunities, and SHALL preserve competing Opportunity Hypotheses until evidence supports adjudication.
- **AR-1506** — Opportunity Formation SHALL preserve grounding Assessments and Products, participating domains, worldview version, confidence, and uncertainty, and SHALL remain scientifically explainable.
- **AR-1507** — Genesis SHALL synthesize opportunities across domains while preserving the contribution of each participating domain.
- **AR-1508** — Genesis SHALL assess Opportunity Timing, grounded in emergence, readiness, and time-to-inflection understanding, preserving uncertainty.
- **AR-1509** — Genesis SHALL assess Opportunity Magnitude, grounded in understanding and always carrying explicit uncertainty; no magnitude SHALL be asserted as certain.
- **AR-1510** — Opportunity lifecycle state values and semantics SHALL be owned by Genesis, while the structural lifecycle field remains owned by the Knowledge Model; every state transition SHALL become a historical event.
- **AR-1511** — Genesis SHALL continuously adjudicate Opportunity Hypotheses as understanding evolves, preserving complete grounding and justification for every adjudication.
- **AR-1512** — When belief revision or validation drift changes the understanding grounding an Opportunity, that Opportunity SHALL be re-evaluated; no Opportunity SHALL silently retain a superseded grounding.
- **AR-1513** — Every Opportunity SHALL be replayable, binding the versions of understanding it was formed against; prior versions SHALL be preserved and remain replayable.
- **AR-1514** — Opportunity Portfolios SHALL be coordinated collections of evolving opportunities; they SHALL carry no allocation, position, or capital and SHALL NOT be investment portfolios.
- **AR-1515** — Genesis SHALL discover opportunities continuously, not on demand.
- **AR-1516** — Genesis SHALL provide Opportunity Hypotheses and Opportunity Portfolios to downstream investment and decision subsystems.
- **AR-1517** — Genesis SHALL discover what is worth pursuing and SHALL NOT decide how to act upon it; it SHALL NOT allocate capital, select securities, take positions, construct investment portfolios, or execute decisions.
- **AR-1518** — Genesis SHALL remain implementation independent.
- **AR-1519** — Genesis SHALL maintain explicit Opportunity Relationships in an Opportunity Graph, relating Opportunities to one another and to the Intelligence Assessments that ground them; the Opportunity Graph SHALL remain continuously evolving and replayable.
- **AR-1520** — Genesis SHALL continuously prioritize Opportunities by expected significance, re-evaluable as understanding evolves; prioritization SHALL express relative significance, not investment recommendation.
